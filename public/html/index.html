HEY THERE! WELCOME TO POKÉMON WORLD

Hello there! Welcome to the world of POKEMON!

My name is OAK! People call me the POKEMON PROF! This world is inhabited by creatures called POKEMON! For some people, POKEMON are pets. Others use them for fights. Myself...I study POKEMON as a profession.

I need your help to discover all Pokemon in the World. Time ago I used to find all of them and see their characteristics...But I'm too old to do it now. In this world there are a lot of species of Pokemon and I have the dream to discover all of them with this machine named Pokedex.

I will give you one of the three basic Pokemon that will help you in your adventure. You can decide what you want to do, but every time you see and catch a Pokemon, their info will be saved in the Pokedex. Every one writes their own path.

You will pass through several areas and you will be able to catch wild Pokemon and to battle great opponents. Try to win all battles and became the Champion of the Pokemon World! I will be listening for you! GOOD LUCK!
